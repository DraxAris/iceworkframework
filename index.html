Ice Work Framework quick start

Quick introduction:

You can use the IW (Ice Work) Framework for all your projects. Ranging from a webshop to a newspaper or a portofolio, it doesn't matter IW is open source and can be used by everyone. It can also be adapted by everyone, just sit tight in this short guide you can find all the important stuff, tips, tricks, rules, ... (use F12 to view all the classes)
